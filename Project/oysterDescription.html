Oysters


They are generally whitish-gray in outer shell color, and their inside shell is usually a porcelain white. They have extremely strong adductor muscles to close their shells when threatened. Look for the distinctive cartonette packaging with the easy ‘Zip-Top’ pull ring-tab lid. Each can has a recipe to stimulate your creative juices and help make your next party one to remember. They present many health benefits: they are low in saturated fat, free of trans fat, a good source of protein and an excellent source of iron
