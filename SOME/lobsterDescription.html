Lobster


The lobster at SOME BRAND comes from the waters of Gaspésie, Côte-Nord or the Maritimes (Nova Scotia, New Brunswick and Prince Edward Island). Lobster from Gaspésie is easily recognizable with its blue tag placed on each lobster’s claw, done directly on the boat when fishing. The alphanumeric code found on the blue tag, makes it possible to identify the fisherman on monhomard.ca. The number of days for lobster fishing in Quebec is 68 days, usually starts late April or early May and ends in early or mid-July.
